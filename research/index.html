Research

My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence, high-performance computing, and biomedicine. I develop computational methods to address challenges in cardiovascular disease diagnosis, clinical decision support, and digital health.

Research Areas

AI for Healthcare

Developing AI algorithms and large language models for clinical decision support and personalized health coaching.

Cardiovascular Modeling

Massively parallel computational fluid dynamics for blood flow simulation and disease biomarker discovery.

High Performance Computing

Scalable, memory-efficient algorithms for real-time biomedical applications on supercomputers.

Synthetic Health Data

Generating representative synthetic datasets to enable privacy-preserving AI research in healthcare.

Current Projects

  • AI-driven prediction of functional biomarkers for long COVID using data from the Million Veteran Program and UK Biobank
  • Real-time hemodynamic assessment in coronary bifurcation lesions using simulation-based machine learning
  • Synthetic data generation for training healthcare AI models
  • Development of empathetic large language models for personalized health coaching
